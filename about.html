Our Story

Discover what makes XO FOODS unique.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve bold, high‑quality food that tastes like it was made just for you, whether you’re dining in, grabbing to‑go, or ordering delivery. We believe convenience should never come at the expense of flavor, freshness, or hospitality—and we build every plate around that promise. From our seasoning blends to our frying oil and prep standards, we obsess over the details so every bite hits the spot. We’re here to feed our community quickly, fairly, and consistently, while keeping that warm, neighborhood feel. When you choose XO FOODS, you’re choosing speed with soul—and that’s our north star.

Our Inspiration

XO FOODS was born inside a humble gas station in Waterloo, IA, where great food and real people cross paths every day. We drew inspiration from home‑cooked comfort and the energy of street food—simple, craveable, and made with care. Watching customers light up over crispy chicken and hot, seasoned wedges reminded us that good food doesn’t need fancy surroundings. It needs heart, consistency, and generous portions. That spirit drives us to keep the menu honest, the flavors loud, and the experience welcoming.

Why XO FOODS?

For over a decade, we’ve earned trust one order at a time by cooking fresh, serving fast, and treating everyone like a regular. Our recipes are simple on purpose—quality ingredients, seasoned right, cooked hot, and handed to you with a smile. We’re family‑owned and community‑driven, which means we listen, we adapt, and we never cut corners. The reviews and repeat customers speak for themselves: XO FOODS is where convenience meets real flavor. If you want food that’s reliable, generous, and seriously tasty, you’ll feel right at home here.
